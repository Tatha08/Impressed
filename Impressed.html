To Chayanika – A Heart Speaks

Give me rosogolla melting on my tongue or a plate of kosha mangsho that warms the soul, and I’m in my element. That’s who I am—someone who notices the sweetness, the spice, the little joys that make this world worth being in.

I wonder a lot about life. About the world, about people, about why things are the way they are. Sometimes it feels messy, confusing, even unfair—but I try to understand it, to see the beauty within it. And in all of this, I find meaning in little things, in moments that others might overlook.

Likha bich me kya yeh tumko padhna hai.
